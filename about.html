About Philip Howard

A Sonoma County Native Who Builds Like It's His Own Home

Philip Howard grew up in Sonoma County, and that sense of place shapes everything about how he runs Howard Construction. He is a third-generation builder — his father and grandfather were both carpenters, and Phil credits them with instilling in him both a love of building and a deep respect for the craft.

Phil began working in construction as a teenager, starting in roofing where he developed his hands-on skills and his ability to manage crews and deliver accurate estimates. At 28, he joined a well-established firm specializing in high-end new home construction, remodels, and commercial work — where he refined his commitment to communication and quality workmanship that defines Howard Construction today.

"I learned early that the best construction is invisible. When the job is done right, clients don't notice the work — they just notice how much they love their home."

Howard Construction Inc. is a family-owned business serving Sonoma, Marin, Napa, Mendocino, and Lake Counties. Phil personally oversees every project from first consultation to final punch list — no project managers, no middlemen, no surprises.

Our History

25 Years of Building the North Bay

From Phil's first days in roofing to leading one of Sonoma County's most trusted construction firms.

Early Career
Starting in the Trades

Phil begins working in construction as a teenager, starting in roofing. He develops hands-on skills alongside the ability to supervise crews and produce accurate estimates for clients across Sonoma County.

Late 1990s
High-End Residential Construction

At 28, Phil joins a well-established firm specializing in high-end new home construction, remodels, and commercial work. He refines his understanding of quality workmanship and client communication at scale.

2000
Howard Construction Inc. Founded

Phil founds Howard Construction Inc. in Santa Rosa, CA. The company opens with a commitment to serving Sonoma County homeowners with the same level of quality he provided at the previous firm — but with more personal accountability.

2000s–2010s
Growing Reputation Across the North Bay

Howard Construction grows through word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied clients. The company expands its service area across Sonoma, Marin, Napa, Mendocino, and Lake Counties while maintaining Phil's hands-on oversight model.

2012
Houzz Pro Verified Profile

Howard Construction joins Houzz Pro and begins receiving verified reviews, expanding visibility across Northern California and building a consistent record of 5-star client feedback.

Today
500+ Projects. Same Principles.

With more than 500 completed projects and 25 years in business, Howard Construction continues to operate the same way Phil started — personally involved, transparent, and committed to delivering work that outlasts the contract.
