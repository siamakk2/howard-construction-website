About Philip Howard
The Man Behind Every Project

Philip Howard isn't just the owner of Howard Construction — he is the contractor you hire when you want it done right. Born and raised in Sonoma County, Phil has deep roots in the communities he serves. His grandfather was a carpenter. His father was a carpenter. And from the time Phil was a teenager swinging a hammer on roofing crews, it was clear he was built for this work too.

What separates Phil from the competition isn't just skill — it's character. Over 25 years, he has built a reputation across Sonoma Valley, Napa Valley, Santa Rosa, San Francisco, and Calistoga as the contractor who always shows up, always communicates, and never cuts corners. Clients don't just hire him once — they call him back for the next project, and the one after that.

At 28, Phil joined one of the North Bay's premier high-end construction firms, where he sharpened his craft on complex custom homes and large commercial projects. When he founded Howard Construction in 2000, he brought that high standard to a business that treats every project — no matter the size — with the same level of care, precision, and professionalism.

"My grandfather always said: measure twice, cut once. But the real lesson was this — your reputation is built one nail at a time. I've never forgotten that."

— Philip Howard, Owner & President

Today, Howard Construction is one of the most trusted names in Northern California residential construction. Every project is personally overseen by Phil — from the first phone call to the final walkthrough. That is a promise, not a marketing line.

Our History
25 Years of Building the North Bay
Early Career
Born Into the Trades

Philip Howard grows up in Sonoma County surrounded by builders — his grandfather and father both carpenters. As a teenager he starts working roofing crews across the North Bay, developing hands-on skills and a natural ability to manage people and deliver accurate estimates.

Late 1990s
High-End Residential Construction

At 28, Phil joins one of the North Bay's premier construction firms specializing in high-end custom homes, large remodels, and commercial projects. He manages complex builds across Sonoma Valley, Napa, and Marin — refining his craft and his conviction that client communication is just as important as construction quality.

2000
Howard Construction Inc. Founded

Phil founds Howard Construction Inc. in Santa Rosa with a simple mission: bring the quality of high-end residential construction to every homeowner in Northern California, with more personal accountability than any large firm could offer. He personally oversees every project from day one.

2000s
Building a Reputation Across the North Bay

Howard Construction grows entirely through word-of-mouth referrals. Homeowners in Santa Rosa, Sonoma Valley, Napa, Petaluma, and Healdsburg pass Phil's name to their neighbors, their family, and their friends. Not a single dollar is spent on advertising — the work speaks for itself.

2010s
Expanding Into Napa Valley, Calistoga & San Francisco

Client referrals bring Howard Construction into new markets — Napa Valley estates, Calistoga wine country homes, and selected San Francisco residences. The company earns Houzz Pro verification and begins accumulating the five-star review record it holds today.

2017–2020
Fire Rebuild and Recovery Work

Following the devastating North Bay fires, Howard Construction plays an important role in helping families rebuild in Santa Rosa, Sonoma Valley, and surrounding communities. Phil and his team bring the same care and precision to fire rebuilds as to any custom project — helping clients not just rebuild, but build better.

Today
500+ Projects. The Same Promise.

With more than 500 completed projects and 25 years in business, Howard Construction continues to operate the same way Phil started — personally involved in every project, transparent with every client, and committed to delivering work that outlasts the contract and exceeds every expectation.
