What We Build

Full-Service Construction
& Remodeling

From a single bathroom to a complete custom build — we manage every detail, permit, and subcontractor so you don't have to. Serving Sonoma County since 2000.

01
🏠

Home Remodels

Complete renovations that respect your budget and your daily routine — including when you're living in the space throughout construction.

  • Kitchen renovations — full gut or cosmetic refresh
  • Bathroom remodels and master suite upgrades
  • Open floor plan conversions and structural work
  • Flooring, drywall, finish carpentry, and paint
  • Window and door replacements
  • Whole-home renovations and upgrades
02
🏗️

Home Additions

Seamlessly integrated additions that match your home's existing architecture and significantly increase property value.

  • Single-story and second-story room additions
  • ADU construction (detached and attached)
  • Master bedroom and bath suite additions
  • Garage conversions to living space
  • Sunroom and family room additions
  • In-law suite and guest house construction
03
🏊

Pool & Outdoor Living

Custom pool construction and complete backyard transformations. Authorized Empire Fiberglass Pools dealer for Northern California.

  • Fiberglass pool installation (Empire authorized)
  • Gunite and vinyl pool construction
  • Pool deck and surrounding hardscape
  • Outdoor kitchens and barbecue centers
  • Retaining walls and drainage solutions
  • Complete backyard renovation
04
🪵

Decks & Pergolas

Custom wood, composite, and Trex® deck systems designed for Sonoma County's climate and lifestyle.

  • New deck framing and construction
  • Deck replacement and structural repair
  • Pergolas, arbors, and shade structures
  • Built-in benches, planters, and railings
  • Fencing and privacy screens
  • Concrete patios and walkways
05
🔧

Garages & Utility

Detached garages, utility rooms, and outbuildings built to California code with the same quality as our primary residential work.

  • Detached and attached garage construction
  • Garage rebuilds after damage or demolition
  • Utility room and laundry room builds
  • Workshop and storage structure construction
  • Garage door replacement and framing
  • Electrical and plumbing rough-in
06
🏡

ADU Construction

California has streamlined ADU permits significantly. We guide you from site assessment through design, permits, construction, and final sign-off.

  • Detached ADU new construction
  • Attached ADU additions
  • Garage conversion to ADU
  • Junior ADU (JADU) within existing home
  • Full permit management with Sonoma County
  • Utility hookups and separate metering
